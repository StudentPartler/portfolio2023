About Me

Hello, I am about to tell you a little bit about myself, my name is Eric Niee, I firmly believe in setting ambitious goals by putting up the necessary effort to achieve them. My story started in the picturesque Mauritius Island, a true paradise with breathtaking beaches surrounded by turquoise sea and welcoming locals.

A few year back, however, I relocated to Dublin, Ireland and started my career in hospitality of the hilton hotel brands. Starting as a trainee, thus, I became familiar with the working environment then I rose up the ladder to finally joined the hotel management team and I worked very hard during my entire stay in the hotel business.

Life, however, can be unpredictable. I found myself in County Longford, far from the bustling city of Dublin and its hotels. With limited job opportunities in the hotel sector, I made a tough but necessary decision to change my career path.

I chose to return to college and recently completed my Bachelor degree in Computer Science (Software Development with Mobile Apps & Connected Devices) at TUS University in Athlone. This degree has opened new doors for me, marking a significant shift from hospitality to technology. It’s an exciting change that promises a bright future.

My journey is all about adaptability, resilience, and the dedication to excel in every task. It’s about embracing challenges, seeking knowledge, and approaching change with an open mind and firm determination to succeed.
